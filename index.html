About Us

About Us

Owners Joe Blogs and Terry Toweling have worked together since 1901 and form an amazing team each side of the kitchen pass.

Restauranteur Joe Blogs, who hails from Merrytown previously ran the much loved and award winning Goku Restaurant in the Fallwell suburb of Ascot.

Ever gracious in his role as host, Terry ensures professional and attentive service in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

Spanish born chef Joe has won over the tastebuds of diners and critics alike with his exciting and innovative approach to asian inspired greek cuisine.

Together with a talented team of chefs, you will be served modern asian inspired greek food at its best.
